About Donatien

"Effective design connects people to their space by personalising their experience. Our projects become unique, with an inspirational rather than merely functional identity."

We are a full-service creative production studio creating and executing one-off designs for our clients. Our studio works across retail, hospitality and corporate portfolios, delivering activations, immersive art experiences, visual merchandising, space transformations, brand and product styling and large-scale floral installations.

As Creative Directors, we develop the creative content and direct the creative process. We are distinctive, flexible, considerate and meticulous, and these principles drive the quality of our work. Every detail is bespoke and purposeful - every element is considered.

We believe in creating emotion-driven work that fits our client's goals and resonates with their audiences.

DUO creative design studio was established in 2013 in Perth, Western Australia.
